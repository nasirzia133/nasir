what is the ielts exam? the ielts exam, or ‘international english language testing system’, is one of the most acknowledged english"language tests. it is widely recognised by companies and universities around the world as an accurate way to test someone’s level of english. it is also the test of choice for many people who are planning to migrate to canada, new zealand, australia or the uk and who need to demonstrate their abilities in english to the immigration departments.
